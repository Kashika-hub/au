Welcome to Anna University

Anna University, established in 1978, stands as one of India’s premier technical universities, nurturing leaders in science, technology, and engineering. About Us

Anna University was established on 4th September 1978 as a unitary type of University. Named after the late Dr. C.N. Annadurai, former Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu, the university was formed by bringing together four renowned technical institutions in Chennai: College of Engineering, Guindy (CEG), Alagappa College of Technology (ACT), Madras Institute of Technology (MIT), and School of Architecture and Planning (SAP).

Over the decades, Anna University has grown into a premier center for higher education, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, technology, architecture, and applied sciences. It is recognized globally for its contributions in research and innovation.

The University has played a key role in fostering industry collaborations, advancing knowledge in cutting-edge areas like artificial intelligence, space technology, biotechnology, and geoinformatics. Today, Anna University continues to inspire thousands of students, equipping them to meet global challenges with confidence and skill.
